Background

01 - 01

Brevis uses its own custom Large Language Model (LLM) rather than relying on an external API, which allows for greater adaptability, security, and control over the data it processes. By developing its own LLM, Brevis can optimize its system for highly specialized tasks and domain-specific insights, ensuring the highest levels of performance and reliability. This in-house approach also reduces potential bottlenecks related to external services, while enabling continual refinements and improvements based on user feedback.
